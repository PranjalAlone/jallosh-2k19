About

Every year students of GCOEA organize a special event called 'Alumni Association'. Alumni Association is association of alumni which graduated 25 years ago from GCOEA.

Every year the event is given a Special name. Last year it was 'Mahol', but this year we decided to give it a name that would reflect this Special event. So, we named it 'जल्लोष' (Jallosh). The word 'Jallosh' is a marathi word meaning Happy Event or Victorious Event. Alumni who graduated 25 years ago from GCOEA meet each other after long time and have fun.

This year alumni who graduated in year 1995 will join us for जल्लोष. The event is scheduled on 28th December 2019.

We request everyone to join us for this Special event. The event will contain interaction session with Alumni where students can ask questions to Alumni, gain knowledge from experienced people and industrial exposure.

Also, if you are an Alumni from Batch 1995 you can register for event by clicking on the button below. This will take you to a form. Fill in the required details and submit the form.
